Step 1. BEFORE proceeding, create a new folder called Switch
            on your desktop.

Step 2. Click [Browse] at the next prompt and navigate to the
             folder you created in Step 1. Do this by scrolling to
             the bottom of the display.

Step 3. Click [Unzip] to proceed with the installation

The installed programs will be in that folder.
